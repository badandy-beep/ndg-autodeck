V22 CHANGES FROM V21
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. SLIDE CONSOLIDATION: 30 slides → 22 slides
   - Slide 5: Crisis + Winter merged
   - Slide 7: AI Revolution + Contrast pairs merged
   - Slide 8: AI Wisdom + Convergence equation merged
   - Slide 10: Warriors + Armor + Scripture merged
   - Slide 20: Selectivity + Principles merged
   - Slide 22: CTA + Finale merged

2. KARAOKE ENHANCEMENTS:
   - Opacity fade-in (0 → 1)
   - Scale animation (0.95 → 1)
   - Pulse effect on key emphasis words (WISDOM, WINTER IS HERE, etc.)
   - Slower transitions (500ms) for smoother reveals

3. IMAGE SIZES INCREASED:
   - Hero: 320px → 400px
   - Large (St. Michael): 380px → 450px
   - Medium: 300px → 380px
   - Small: 240px → 300px

4. MOBILE: Full responsive version (was placeholder)
   - Touch gesture navigation (swipe left/right)
   - Scaled fonts (20-30% smaller)
   - 2-column grids instead of 3-column
   - Larger touch targets (44px minimum)
   - 20% longer slide durations

5. SERVICE SLIDES: Added "Powered by Socratic wisdom" tie-in

6. TIMING: Enhanced absorption pauses (1500ms), faster logo cycling (2500ms)
